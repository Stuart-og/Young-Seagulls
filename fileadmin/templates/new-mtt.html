Michael Poke

Goalkeeper

Michael Poke joined the club in the summer of 2010 on a Bosman free transfer. The 6ft 1ins former Southampton goali impressed Gus Poyet while on loan at Torquay United, where he helped them win promotion from the Blue Square Premier via the playoffs in 2009, then he played a major role in helping them stay in League Two the following season.
